# credits-tracker
The app will let students enter their classes one by one. 
<img width="1440" alt="Screen Shot 2022-05-01 at 10 38 46 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/46438303/166178113-8d7085d6-af69-4b5f-a5d9-d145141e6fbc.png">

Lastly, the app will let users delete classes from their list as they complete them.
